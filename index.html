The August 2023 Wildfires

In early August 2023, a series of wildfires broke out in the Hawaiian Islands. Of all the islands, Maui was the most impacted and continues to be deeply affected in the aftermath. Many families have been left without shelter and essential resources.

Data:

Hawaii's Department of Business, Economic Development & Tourism reports:

  • 100+ lives lost
  • 2000+ homes destroyed
  • 800+ businesses destroyed
  • 7000+ people left unemployed
